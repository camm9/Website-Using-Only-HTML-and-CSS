Meet the Team

Marcio Antony

Marcio is a Brazilian blackbelt currently living in Vancouver, Canada. He started training Jiu Jitsu as a child at his father's gym. He went on to teach Jiu Jitsu at gyms in Brazil, USA, and Canada. His favourite competitor to watch is Leandro Lo and his favourite submission is the triangle.

Catherine Methven

Catherine is a Canadian-South African currently living in Vancouver, Canada. She started training in 2018 and currently is a purple belt. She loves training no-gi and watching instructionals. Her favourite competitor to watch is Gordon Ryan and her favourite submission is the kimura.

Resenha was started in 2021 by Marcio and Catherine. They wanted to create an online resource for Jiu Jitsu that would cover topics and knowledge that weren't widely talked about and had only been shared by word of mouth previously. The goal of Resenha is to share all Jiu Jitsu knowledge freely and to make Jiu Jitsu one of the most popular sports in the world.
