Help Children Learn Jiu Jitsu in South Africa!

Resenha BJJ supports a non-profit organization that brings Jiu Jitsu to township communities in Cape Town, South Africa. With this program we bring Jiu Jitsu mats directly to areas where kids do not have the resources to pursue afterschool activities. The goal of this program is teach kids about sportsmanship, respect, self-defence, and anti-bullying.

However this program is very costly to run. All donations go towards to following needs:

  • Fuel to transport mats and instructors
  • Instructor wages ($25 USD or R400 per Class)
  • Mat cleaning supplies
  • Jiu Jitsu belts for Students
  • Care packages for students (basic neccessities students can take home)
  • Afterschool snack for students
